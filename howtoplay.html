HOW TO PLAY

1. Lay the game mat on the ground. All participants must have their shoes off before they step onto the mat. Each participant starts on a different side of the mat.

2. Before you begin,one player has to be chosen to be the referee. They decide who starts and spins the wheel. The referee can join in although it is easier if they watch from the side. The referee is in charge of the spinner that tells players which part of the body has to be placed on the mat.

3. Players must follow the instruction of the spinner in order to progress. It's done by placing the required body part onto the nearest available circle of the correct color.

4. If there are no open spaces, spin the spinner again. If your hand or foot is already on the required circle you must move it to a different spot. Players cannot move their hand or feet that are not chosen by the spinner if they already occupy a circle on the game mat.

5.If at any point a player’s arm or knee touches the mat or if a player falls over then eliminate them from the game.

6. Players can remove an arm or knee if it’s necessary for moving a body part to another position on the mat.

7. Players must replace their hand or foot before completing their turn.

8. The last person standing wins the round.
